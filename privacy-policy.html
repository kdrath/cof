Overview

The Center for Operational Foresight ("we," "us," or "our") is an independent publication operated by Kim Drath. This policy explains what information we collect when you interact with this website or subscribe to our publication, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

We collect only what we need. We do not sell your information. We do not advertise.

Information you provide. When you subscribe to the Center for Operational Foresight newsletter or mailing list, we collect your email address. If you contact us directly, we may retain the content of that communication and any contact details you include.

Information collected automatically. This information is used in aggregate to understand how the site is used and to maintain its operation.

Substack. Our publication is hosted and distributed through Substack. If you subscribe, create an account, or interact with our content on Substack, Substack's own privacy practices apply in addition to this policy. We encourage you to review Substack's Privacy Policy at substack.com/privacy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to deliver our publication to subscribers, respond to correspondence, maintain and improve the website, and understand general readership patterns in aggregate.

We do not use your information to build advertising profiles, sell to third parties, or for any purpose unrelated to operating this publication.

Email Communications

If you subscribe, you will receive issues of the publication and occasional notices about the Center's work. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email or by contacting us directly. Unsubscribing removes you from future mailings; it does not delete records already held by Substack, which is governed by their policies.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with service providers who assist in operating this publication, including Substack and standard web hosting and analytics providers, only to the extent necessary to operate the service. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of the publication and its readers.

Data Retention

Subscriber information is retained for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your email address may be retained in suppression lists to honor your opt-out preference. Correspondence is retained for a reasonable period necessary to address your inquiry.

Because we collect minimal personal data and do not engage in data sales or targeted advertising, many of these provisions have limited practical application to our operations, but we take them seriously and will honor requests to the extent required.
