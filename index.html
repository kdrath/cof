Director's Note

Most foresight looks outward, toward markets and emerging trends. The Center looks at how organizations respond once those forces enter them: how decisions move through reporting lines, incentives, and authority structures.

The work connects strategic foresight with operational reality, bringing together pieces that exist across many fields.

The future is already visible in the present. I find that endlessly fascinating. This is how I explore it.

— Kim D., Executive Director
